About Us

EcoRoots is more than an online shop; it's a commitment to sustainable living.

Living a sustainable lifestyle is a powerful choice that reverberates beyond individual actions, shaping the future of our planet. By embracing practices that prioritize environmental well-being, such as reducing waste, choosing renewable resources, and supporting eco-friendly products, we contribute to a collective effort for a healthier Earth. Adopting sustainable habits in our daily lives not only minimises our ecological footprint but also fosters a sense of responsibility and connection to the environment. From conscious consumption to mindful waste management, each sustainable decision becomes a catalyst for positive change, paving the way for a greener, more harmonious coexistence between humanity and the planet we call home.

The journey towards a sustainable lifestyle is a transformative and purposeful endeavor, aligning personal choices with a profound commitment to the well-being of our global ecosystems.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to make mindful choices for a greener future.

Our Products

At EcoRoots, we curate a thoughtfully selected range of eco-friendly products, from zero-waste essentials to consciously crafted goods. All our products are sourced from suppliers within the UK and fall under at least one of these categories: Organic, Natural, Sustainable, Zero-Waste, Handmade, Vegan. Join us on the journey towards sustainability and discover the world of eco-conscious living at EcoRoots - where every purchase makes a positive impact on the planet.

Our Packaging

In line with our values, we strive to use the most sustainable packaging available. We aim to offer products that come in plastic free and eco-friendly packaging but we cannot always control our suppliers packaging. However, we ensure all orders are delivered in eco-friendly, plastic-free packaging. Typically, our packaging will consist of one or more of the following elements:

Mailer: We use 100% compostable mailers made from a combination of biodegradable polymers and polyester from renewable biomass. They can be re-used or composted and disappear in 6 months.

Tissue Paper: Some items are delivered wrapped in tissue paper for more protection, this paper is made from recycled materials, acid free and FSC certified.

Boxes: Our boxes are completely recyclable. However, we encourage you to keep them to reduce waste. They can be reused for a variety of purposes. The tape used on our boxes is recyclable and made from an environmentally friendly paper based formula.

Labels: The labels in our packaging are 100% made from post consumer waste paper and completely biodegradable.
